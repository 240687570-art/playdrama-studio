# Competitor Analysis

## Yoroll

Positioning: AI-native interactive video game platform.

Strengths:

- Clear interactive video direction
- Low-code creation story
- Good fit for playable short video concepts

Likely gaps:

- Creator monetization depth is unclear
- Real gameplay systems may be shallow
- Role and world consistency will be hard
- Platform distribution and marketplace need time

## Differentiation Strategy

PlayDrama Studio should win on:

- Character consistency
- Game state and variables
- Genre templates for proven short drama formats
- H5 publishing and analytics
- Paid endings and creator monetization
- Marketplace for templates, roles, and components

## Initial Beachhead

Start with suspense interactive drama because it naturally supports:

- Hooks
- Clues
- Branches
- Password puzzles
- Hidden endings
- Paid reveal points
