# Development Roadmap

## Operating Model

The project is run by an AI-first commercial team. Each phase must produce product progress, business learning, and an updated backlog.

Core team owners:

- AI CEO: strategy and priority decisions
- AI Product Manager: scope and user workflows
- AI Tech Lead: architecture and release quality
- AI Frontend Engineer: creator studio and player
- AI Backend Engineer: persistence, publishing, analytics, payments
- AI Agent Engineer: AI generation and orchestration
- AI Content Director: templates and demo quality
- AI Growth Lead: creator acquisition and launch
- AI Creator Success: onboarding and feedback
- AI Monetization Lead: pricing and revenue tests
- AI Compliance Lead: safety, rights, and policy

## Phase 0: Validation, 2 Weeks

Owner: AI CEO + AI Product Manager + AI Growth Lead

Deliverables:

- Competitor analysis
- 10 genre templates
- 20 creator interviews
- Figma or coded prototype
- MVP PRD
- Pricing assumptions

Validation targets:

- 30 creator signups
- 10 paid beta candidates
- 3 creators willing to provide real scripts or materials

Commercial exit criteria:

- One clear beachhead market
- One launch genre selected
- First beta creator list

## Phase 1: MVP, 6 Weeks

Owner: AI Product Manager + AI Tech Lead + AI Frontend Engineer

Deliverables:

- User login
- Project dashboard
- AI story generation
- Character library
- Story graph editor
- Variables and conditions v1
- Interaction components v1
- H5 preview player
- Publish links
- Basic analytics

Engineering goal:

- A creator can generate, edit, preview, and publish one playable suspense drama.

Commercial exit criteria:

- 3 internal demo works
- 10 beta creators ready to test
- First creator onboarding playbook

## Phase 2: Content Quality, 8 Weeks

Owner: AI Agent Engineer + AI Content Director

Deliverables:

- Character consistency controls
- World bible
- Shot script generator
- Storyboard editor
- Voice generation
- Subtitle generation
- Cover generator
- Template library v1
- Story quality checker

Key metrics:

- Average publishable work creation time under 30 minutes
- Creator publish rate above 40%
- Completion rate above 35%

Commercial exit criteria:

- 10 public-quality demo works
- Template library ready for creator beta
- Generation cost tracked per publishable work

## Phase 3: Monetization, 8 Weeks

Owner: AI Monetization Lead + AI Backend Engineer + AI Creator Success

Deliverables:

- Paid chapters
- Paid endings
- Rewarded ads
- Creator tipping
- Template marketplace
- Character asset marketplace
- Creator profile
- Ranking pages
- A/B tests for covers and opening scenes

Key metrics:

- Paid conversion above 3%
- Creator D7 retention above 25%
- Template reuse above 20%

Commercial exit criteria:

- Paid ending test live
- Pricing tiers drafted
- Creator revenue share model drafted

## Phase 4: Global Platform, 3-6 Months

Owner: AI CEO + AI Growth Lead + AI Compliance Lead

Deliverables:

- Chinese, English, Japanese, and Korean
- WeChat mini program export
- TikTok playable export
- Android APK packaging
- Steam demo packaging
- Team collaboration
- IP licensing center
- Creator revenue share
- Content moderation

Commercial exit criteria:

- Public launch page
- Demo gallery
- Creator marketplace v1
- Safety and takedown workflow
- Global creator acquisition plan
